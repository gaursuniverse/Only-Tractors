Oldest Tractor

We started with a Ford model 8N tractor, and production began in July 1947 with the 4-speed transmission and was invented by Harry Ferguson. The transmission system of the Ford model 8N tractor is reliable and smooth. It has a powerful and reliable hydraulic system. The main feature of this tractor is a four-cylinder L head engine, and maximum drawbar 23.16hp on second gear, 3.188in cylinder bore, 3.75in stroke, constant mesh transmission. These are unique features that make it the most popular farm tractor for all time. It was the first Ford tractor which had a clutch on the left side and independent brakes on the right side. The main special feature of this tractor is its design that reduces the possibility of external oil leakage and reduces repair costs. The price of this tractor is $1404(1952).
